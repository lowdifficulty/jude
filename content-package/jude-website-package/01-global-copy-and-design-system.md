# Jude Global Copy and Design System

## Brand Idea
Jude is the wall-mounted AI companion for Home, Health, and Happiness.

## One-Line Positioning
Jude helps elderly adults live safer, healthier, happier, and more independent lives at home while giving families peace of mind.

## Tagline Options
- Home, Health, and Happiness on the wall.
- A little more peace in the house.
- Help at home, with a human touch.
- The friendliest wall in the house.
- Made for Mom. Built for peace of mind.
- Keeps the house helpful.
- The home companion families can trust.

## Tone
Warm, capable, calm, confident, neighborly, family-first, and simple.

## Southern Charm Keywords
Porch light, family kitchen, Sunday morning, neighborly, steady hand, familiar voice, homegrown, front porch, fresh coffee, family photos, warm wood, gentle sunlight, heirloom frame, dinner table, quiet confidence.

## Apple-Inspired Web Structure
Use short, polished section headlines:
- Home feels easier.
- Health feels calmer.
- Family feels closer.
- Help is always in reach.
- A smarter home, with a softer touch.
- Not just safer. Happier.

## Visual Direction
- Wall-mounted frame in kitchen, hallway, bedroom, living room, or near front door.
- Warm home interiors, not futuristic labs.
- Morning coffee, family photos, porch views, pets, flowers, quilts, wood trim.
- Product screen should show simple UI: time, family photo, today’s schedule, medication reminder, call button.
- Avoid hospital imagery unless showing telehealth gently.

## Color Direction
Keep approved landing page branding. If expanding palette, consider:
- Warm white
- Cream
- Deep navy
- Soft blue
- Sage green
- Warm brass
- Clay / terracotta accents
- Natural wood

## Button Copy
- Meet Jude
- Join the waitlist
- Explore benefits
- See how it works
- Bring Jude home
- Help Mom stay home
- Give Dad a safer day

## Compliance Language
Use:
- Jude helps support medication routines.
- Jude can integrate with compatible devices.
- Jude can alert approved contacts.
- Jude supports care coordination.

Avoid:
- Jude prevents falls.
- Jude diagnoses illness.
- Jude guarantees safety.
- Jude replaces caregivers.
- Jude monitors everything.
