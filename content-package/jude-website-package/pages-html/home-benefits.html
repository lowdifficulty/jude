A home that feels easier, safer, and more helpful.

Jude brings practical support into the rooms where daily life happens. It helps older adults control the home, remember household tasks, stay safer at night, and ask for help without searching for a phone.

Smart home without the headache

Jude can make lights, locks, thermostats, cameras, sensors, and routines easier to use through voice and a large wall-mounted screen.

Instead of asking seniors to manage multiple apps, Jude becomes the simple household hub.

A safer daily rhythm

Morning and bedtime routines can support lighting, door locks, medication reminders, weather, schedule, and safety prompts.

The goal is a home that quietly helps before a problem happens.

Household memory

Jude can remind seniors about trash day, mail, filters, bills, groceries, pet care, and maintenance tasks.

It helps preserve independence while making family support easier.
