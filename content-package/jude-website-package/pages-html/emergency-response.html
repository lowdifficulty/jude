Emergency Response

Help should be simple to ask for.

During an emergency, complicated technology is the wrong answer. Jude is designed to make help easier to request, easier to escalate, and easier for family to understand.

Voice access when reaching a phone is hard

A senior can ask Jude for help from across the room.

This matters during falls, dizziness, confusion, pain, or fear.

Escalation paths

Jude can contact approved family members, caregivers, neighbors, or emergency services depending on setup.

The escalation path should be clear before it is needed.

Useful context

Jude can share what happened, when the alert started, and whether the senior responded.

Families need calm, useful information in urgent moments.
