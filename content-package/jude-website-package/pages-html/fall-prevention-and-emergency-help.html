Fall Prevention and Emergency Help

Help should be easy to ask for, especially when reaching a phone is hard.

Jude is a wall-mounted AI companion designed to make fall prevention and emergency help easier, warmer, and more connected for older adults at home.

Voice-first emergency support

A senior should not have to find a phone in an emergency.

With Jude, the home can respond to simple phrases like asking for help or calling a family member.

Emergency flows can escalate to approved contacts or emergency services depending on the setup.

A safer path through the house

Jude can connect to lights, motion sensors, and smart home routines.

Nighttime path lighting can help reduce dark hallway trips.

Gentle reminders can encourage walkers, canes, shoes, or grab bars.

When something seems wrong

If connected sensors suggest a fall or long period of inactivity, Jude can check in.

If there is no response, it can alert family or caregivers.

This gives families a faster way to know when attention may be needed.

Key benefits

  • Easy voice access to help
  • Supports nighttime safety
  • Can integrate with fall-detection tools
  • Reduces time alone after an emergency
  • Encourages safer movement
  • Reassures distant family

FAQs

Does Jude detect falls by itself?

Jude can be positioned as a hub that works with compatible sensors, cameras, radar, wearables, or other fall-detection tools.

Can Jude call family first?

Yes. Escalation can be designed to contact family, caregivers, neighbors, or emergency services based on family preference.
