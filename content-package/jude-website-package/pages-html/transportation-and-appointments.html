Transportation and Appointments

Jude is a wall-mounted AI companion designed to make transportation and appointments easier, warmer, and more connected for older adults at home.

Appointments are more than calendar events

A doctor visit may require forms, medication lists, transportation, questions, and follow-up plans.

Jude can help organize those steps in plain language.

This reduces the stress around getting out the door.

Ride reminders

Jude can remind seniors when a ride is coming and when it is time to get ready.

Family can be notified if transportation needs help.

No one has to rely on memory alone.

After the visit

Jude can remind seniors about new instructions, future appointments, lab work, or medication changes.

It helps carry the appointment back into daily life.

That is where follow-through happens.

FAQs

Can Jude help schedule rides?

Jude can be designed to coordinate ride reminders, family pickups, medical transportation, or rideshare support depending on integrations.

Can Jude remind family too?

Yes. Approved family contacts can receive appointment or transportation-related updates.
