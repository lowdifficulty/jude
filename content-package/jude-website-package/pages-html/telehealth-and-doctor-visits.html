Telehealth and Doctor Visits

Make every doctor visit easier to prepare for and easier to remember.

Jude is a wall-mounted AI companion designed to make telehealth and doctor visits easier, warmer, and more connected for older adults at home.

Join the Jude waitlist

Before the appointment

Jude can remind seniors when an appointment is coming up and when it is time to leave.

It can help collect recent symptoms, questions, and concerns.

Family members can feel more prepared instead of scrambling at the last minute.

During virtual care

A wall-mounted screen can make telehealth feel less awkward than a small phone.

Jude can help launch visits, show reminders, and support hands-free conversation.

For seniors with vision or dexterity challenges, a larger screen matters.

After the appointment

Doctor instructions are easy to forget after a long visit.

Jude can help store follow-up steps, medication changes, therapy reminders, and lab dates.

Care does not end when the visit ends.

FAQs

Can Jude replace a doctor visit?

No. Jude helps with preparation, reminders, communication, and follow-through.

Can Jude help with telehealth?

Yes. Jude can be designed to make video visits easier with a large, familiar, hands-free screen.
