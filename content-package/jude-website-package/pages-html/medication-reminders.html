Medication Reminders

Gentle medication support that does not feel like nagging.

Jude is a wall-mounted AI companion designed to make medication reminders easier, warmer, and more connected for older adults at home.

Join the Jude waitlist

A calm reminder at the right time

Jude can remind an older adult when it is time to take medication.

The reminder can be spoken aloud and shown clearly on the screen.

For seniors who miss phone alerts, a wall-mounted reminder is much harder to overlook.

Confirmation without confusion

Jude can ask whether the dose was taken and log the response.

If a dose is missed or not confirmed, family or caregivers can be notified.

This helps reduce the repeated question: Did Mom take her medicine today?

Refill support

Jude can remind families when medication may be running low.

It can help organize pharmacy pickups, deliveries, or calls.

The goal is fewer gaps and fewer stressful last-minute refill problems.

Key benefits

  • Reduces missed doses
  • Helps prevent double-dose confusion
  • Supports caregiver visibility
  • Simplifies refill routines
  • Creates a consistent daily rhythm
  • Gives seniors more confidence

FAQs

Can Jude track multiple medications?

Yes. Jude can be structured around morning, afternoon, evening, and bedtime routines for multiple medications.

Can caregivers see missed doses?

Yes. Jude can be designed to notify approved family members or caregivers when important medication routines are missed.
