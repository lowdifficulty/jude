# Jude

## Home, Health, and Happiness on the wall.

Jude is a warm, wall-mounted AI companion for older adults and the families who love them. It helps with daily routines, medication reminders, family connection, smart home control, safety check-ins, and the little moments that make a house feel like home.

Phones get lost. Tablets die. Apps get confusing. Jude stays where life happens — on the wall, easy to see, easy to hear, and easy to talk to.

## Home

### A smarter home that feels simple.

Jude can connect the home around the person who lives there. Lights, locks, thermostat, cameras, reminders, and routines become easier to manage with voice and a large screen.

**Benefit tiles:**

1. **Lights without walking across the room**  
   Turn lights on, dim them, or create safer nighttime paths.

2. **A calmer bedtime**  
   Lock doors, dim lights, confirm medication, and review tomorrow.

3. **Know who is at the door**  
   See visitors and speak to them without getting up.

4. **A home that checks in**  
   Get alerts for water leaks, open doors, unusual activity, or missed routines.
