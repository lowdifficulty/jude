## The worry between phone calls

Families often worry most when they do not know what is happening.

Jude can provide useful check-ins around medication, meals, activity, mood, and appointments.

This helps adult children stay informed without constant interruption.

## Care coordination that feels human

Siblings, caregivers, and family friends can work from the same basic routine plan.

Jude can help create a shared picture of the day.

Everyone knows what matters, who is helping, and what still needs attention.

**Image direction:** Warm home setting with Jude on the wall, natural light, family details, and a calm senior-friendly interface.

## Important alerts, not noise

The goal is not to overwhelm families with every small detail.

Jude should surface meaningful changes: missed medication, no morning response, unusual inactivity, confusion, or emergency requests.

Peace of mind comes from useful signals, not constant surveillance.

## FAQs

### Will Jude notify family all day?
Jude can be designed around meaningful alerts instead of constant updates, so families get information that matters.

### Can more than one caregiver be included?
Yes. Jude can support approved contacts such as adult children, professional caregivers, neighbors, or family friends.

### Is Jude meant to replace family or professional care?
No. Jude is designed to support seniors, families, and caregivers with reminders, communication, coordination, and home routines.
