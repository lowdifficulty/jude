## FAQs

### Can Jude support religious routines?
Yes. Jude can be customized around the senior’s own faith traditions, services, readings, prayers, or community events.

### Can Jude help with hobbies?
Yes. Jude can remember hobbies and suggest simple activities, reminders, or entertainment based on those interests.

### Is Jude meant to replace family or professional care?
No. Jude is designed to support seniors, families, and caregivers with reminders, communication, coordination, and home routines.
