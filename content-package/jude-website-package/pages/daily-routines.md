## FAQs

### Can routines be customized?
Yes. Jude should be built around each person’s schedule, habits, and family preferences.

### Can Jude repeat reminders?
Yes. Important reminders can repeat until acknowledged or escalate to a caregiver when appropriate.

### Is Jude meant to replace family or professional care?
No. Jude is designed to support seniors, families, and caregivers with reminders, communication, coordination, and home routines.
