## FAQs

### Who uses the dashboard?
Approved family members, caregivers, or care coordinators can use the dashboard depending on the family setup.

### Can the dashboard be simple?
Yes. It should prioritize calm summaries, important alerts, and clear next actions.

### Is Jude meant to replace family or professional care?
No. Jude is designed to support seniors, families, and caregivers with reminders, communication, coordination, and home routines.
