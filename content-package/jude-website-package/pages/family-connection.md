## FAQs

### Can Jude display family photos?
Yes. Photo display is one of the most emotionally important features because it makes Jude feel like part of the family home.

### Can seniors send messages back?
Yes. Jude can support simple dictated replies so seniors do not have to type on a phone.

### Is Jude meant to replace family or professional care?
No. Jude is designed to support seniors, families, and caregivers with reminders, communication, coordination, and home routines.
