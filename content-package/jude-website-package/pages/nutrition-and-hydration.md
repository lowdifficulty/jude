## Meals are part of safety

Skipping meals can affect energy, mood, medication routines, and overall health.

Jude can remind seniors when it is time for breakfast, lunch, dinner, or a snack.

Family can be alerted if meals are repeatedly skipped.

## Hydration made simple

Older adults may not always feel thirsty or remember to drink enough water.

Jude can offer gentle reminders throughout the day.

## FAQs

### Can Jude help with special diets?
Jude can store family-provided preferences like low-sodium, diabetic-friendly, soft foods, or favorite meals, while medical diet advice should come from providers.

### Can family help manage groceries?
Yes. Voice-built grocery lists can be shared with approved family members or caregivers.

### Is Jude meant to replace family or professional care?
No. Jude is designed to support seniors, families, and caregivers with reminders, communication, coordination, and home routines.
