FAQs

Can Jude work with existing smart home devices?

The website can position Jude as a smart home hub designed to integrate with compatible lights, locks, thermostats, sensors, cameras, and plugs.

Why wall-mounted instead of a speaker?

A wall-mounted screen is visible, stable, hard to misplace, easier to read, and feels like part of the home.
