For many seniors, a pet is family, companionship, routine, and joy.

Jude can help keep pet care on schedule without making the senior feel managed.

Feeding, walking, water, medication, and vet visits can all be easier to remember.

A healthier rhythm

Pet reminders can also encourage senior movement and daily structure.

A walk, fresh water, or feeding time becomes part of the household rhythm.

Jude helps protect both the senior and the pet.

Family visibility

Jude can keep everyone aware of what has been done and what needs attention.
