Home Safety and Security

A safer home without making it feel like a facility.

Jude is a wall-mounted AI companion designed to make home safety and security easier, warmer, and more connected for older adults at home.

Join the Jude waitlist

The house can speak up

Jude can help the home announce important safety issues clearly.

Open doors, unlocked locks, smoke alarms, water leaks, and unusual activity can become easier to notice.

The goal is calm awareness, not fear.

See who is at the door

Doorbell camera integration can show visitors on Jude’s screen.

Seniors can speak without getting up or opening the door.

This helps reduce risk from strangers, scams, and unwanted visitors.

Evening peace of mind

Jude can run a nightly home check: doors locked, lights set, thermostat comfortable, reminders complete.

That simple closing ritual can help seniors and families sleep better.

A safer night starts with a calmer routine.

FAQs

Can Jude connect to security devices?

Jude can be positioned as a hub for compatible locks, cameras, alarms, sensors, and security systems.

Will it feel invasive?

The brand direction should emphasize choice, privacy, and supportive alerts instead of constant monitoring.
