FAQs

Is an AI companion appropriate for seniors?

Jude should be positioned as supportive companionship and connection, not a replacement for family, friends, caregivers, or clinical support.

Can Jude remember favorite things?

Yes. Jude can remember preferences like favorite music, routines, family names, hobbies, and conversation topics.
