Health Check-Ins

Small daily check-ins can reveal big changes early.

Jude is a wall-mounted AI companion designed to make health check-ins easier, warmer, and more connected for older adults at home.

Join the Jude waitlist

A simple morning question

Jude can start the day with a gentle check-in: How are you feeling today?

The answer can help family notice pain, dizziness, poor sleep, low appetite, or confusion.

Over time, small changes become easier to see.

Health support without a clinic feeling

The experience should feel like a caring conversation, not a medical chart.

Jude can ask plain-language questions and keep routines moving.

Families get better context while seniors still feel at home.

Trends that help conversations

Before doctor visits, Jude can help summarize recent symptoms and routines.

This gives families better notes and helps seniors remember what happened.

The result is a more useful conversation with care providers.

FAQs

Does Jude diagnose health problems?

No. Jude should not be positioned as diagnosing. It supports reminders, tracking, family communication, and care conversations.

Can Jude summarize symptoms?

Yes. Jude can collect user-reported symptoms and create simple summaries for family or appointments.
