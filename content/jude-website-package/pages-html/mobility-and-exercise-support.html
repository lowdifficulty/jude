Mobility and Exercise Support

Keep the body moving gently and safely.

Jude can support mobility through encouragement, routine, and safer home cues. The goal is not intense fitness. It is steady, age-friendly movement that supports daily life.

Join the Jude waitlist

Gentle movement prompts

Jude can remind seniors to stand, stretch, walk, or do simple chair exercises.

Small movements throughout the day can make routines feel more active.

Safer movement cues

Jude can remind someone to use a cane, walker, shoes, grab bars, or nighttime lights.

These cues can support safer habits around the home.

Physical therapy follow-through

After appointments, Jude can remind seniors about provider-recommended exercises or therapy tasks.

Follow-through is easier when reminders are built into the day.

  • Walking reminders

  • Physical therapy prompts
