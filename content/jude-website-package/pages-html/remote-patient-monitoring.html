FAQs

What devices could Jude work with?

Jude can be designed to integrate with compatible cuffs, scales, glucose meters, thermometers, pulse oximeters, and other health devices.

Does Jude interpret readings?

Jude should support tracking and alerts based on configured thresholds, while clinical interpretation remains with licensed professionals.
