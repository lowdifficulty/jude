Jude is a wall-mounted AI companion designed to make fraud and scam protection easier, warmer, and more connected for older adults at home.

FAQs

Can Jude block every scam?

No. Jude should be presented as a helpful layer of education, reminders, and family confirmation, not a guarantee.

What scams can Jude warn about?

Common categories include fake bank alerts, fake Medicare calls, package messages, IRS threats, tech support scams, romance scams, and family emergency scams.
