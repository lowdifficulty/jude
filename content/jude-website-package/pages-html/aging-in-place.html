Home should still feel like home

Jude is designed for the place people already love most: their own home.

It supports the routines that make home feel familiar, from morning coffee to evening lock-up.

Instead of making the house feel clinical, Jude blends in like a trusted family helper on the wall.

A bridge between independence and full-time care

Many families are not ready for assisted living, but they know their parent needs more support.

Jude fills the gap with reminders, check-ins, emergency access, and caregiver updates.

It can start simple and grow as care needs change.

Less pressure on the family

Adult children do not have to wonder all day whether medication was taken, lunch was eaten, or the front door was locked.

Jude helps turn vague worry into useful information.

Families can stay involved without taking over every small part of daily life.

FAQs

Is Jude a replacement for a caregiver?

No. Jude is designed to support families and caregivers, not replace them. It helps with reminders, check-ins, communication, and coordination.

Can Jude grow with changing needs?

Yes. Jude can begin as a simple home companion and later add health reminders, caregiver notifications, and safety integrations.
