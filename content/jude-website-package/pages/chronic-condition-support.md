## Support for the daily routine

Chronic conditions often depend on small habits repeated every day.

Jude can remind seniors to check readings, drink water, move gently, take medication, or prepare for appointments.

It helps make the care plan visible and easier to follow.

## Connected readings and caregiver awareness

Jude can be designed to work with compatible devices like blood pressure cuffs, scales, glucose meters, thermometers, or pulse oximeters.

When readings are logged consistently, families can see patterns more clearly.

Better visibility can lead to earlier conversations with providers.

## FAQs

### What conditions can Jude support?
Jude can support daily routines for common needs like blood pressure tracking, diabetes routines, heart health habits, breathing support, arthritis, mobility, and post-surgery recovery.

### Can Jude connect to health devices?
The product can be built to integrate with compatible remote patient monitoring and consumer health devices.

### Is Jude meant to replace family or professional care?
No. Jude is designed to support seniors, families, and caregivers with reminders, communication, coordination, and home routines.
