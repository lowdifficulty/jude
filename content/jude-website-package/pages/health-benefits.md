# Health Benefits

## Health support that feels gentle, not clinical.

Jude helps turn health routines into simple daily moments. It can remind, encourage, organize, and communicate without making the home feel like a hospital room.

## Medication and routine support

Jude can remind seniors when medication is due, confirm whether it was taken, and alert approved contacts when important routines are missed.

It can also support refill reminders and pharmacy coordination.

**Image direction:** Jude displayed in a warm Southern home setting with clear screen UI, family details, and calm natural light.

## Daily wellness check-ins

Jude can ask about sleep, pain, appetite, hydration, dizziness, mood, and movement.

These check-ins help families notice changes and prepare better questions for providers.

**Image direction:** Jude displayed in a warm Southern home setting with clear screen UI, family details, and calm natural light.

## Telehealth and chronic care

Jude can help seniors prepare for appointments, launch video visits, remember after-visit instructions, and support device-based health routines.

It is designed to help care plans become easier to follow at home.
