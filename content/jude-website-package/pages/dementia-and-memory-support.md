## Gentle orientation

Jude can remind someone of the day, date, time, weather, and schedule.

These simple cues help make the day feel less confusing.

The tone should be calm, familiar, and never frustrated.

## Support for repeated questions

Family members can get worn down answering the same question again and again.

Jude can patiently repeat key answers, like when a daughter is visiting or whether morning medicine was taken.

This protects dignity for the senior and patience for the family.

## FAQs

### Is Jude for dementia care?
Jude can be positioned as supportive technology for families dealing with mild memory issues or cognitive decline, while clinical care remains with professionals.

### Can Jude help with nighttime confusion?
Yes. Jude can provide calming prompts, soft lighting routines, and family alerts if unusual nighttime activity occurs.

### Is Jude meant to replace family or professional care?
No. Jude is designed to support seniors, families, and caregivers with reminders, communication, coordination, and home routines.
